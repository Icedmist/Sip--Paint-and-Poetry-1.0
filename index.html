About The Event
WHERE
VOICES
COME ALIVE

Sip, Paint & Poetry 1.0 is a vibrant one-day creative gathering organized by Poets in Nigeria (PIN) in collaboration with Teemerh Arts Foundation — bringing together poets, painters, musicians and creatives under one roof in Gombe.

Whether you come to speak, to paint, to listen, or simply to sip — you belong here. Expect powerful spoken word, live canvases coming to life, and a community that celebrates expression in all its forms.

"Come sip. Come paint. Come speak. Come create."
